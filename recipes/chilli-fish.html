Chilli Fish

Description

Chilli Fish - all things spicy, tangy and delicious! This Indo-fusion dish is an appetizer that will turn your whole meal around even if it’s just plain dal rice. With flavours that are hot and tantalizing, chilli fish serves as a perfect appetizer. It is incredibly easy to cook and with the recipe we have in store for you, your dish will be ready in a jiffy.

Ingredients

  • 500gm boneless fish cubes
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp turmeric powder,chilli powder,pepper powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1tbsp oil for noodles
  • 150 gm noodles
  • 2 tbsp custurd,cornflour
  • 2 tbsp chilli sauce,tomato sauce
  • 1 chopped onion,chopped capsicum
  • 1 tbsp vinegar,sugar
  • 4 garlic cloves,chopped chilli

Steps

  1. Mix all ingredients listed under marinade well. Add the fish and marinate for about 30 minutes. Coat the marinated fish in cornflour. Deep fry the fish until golden brown. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add garlic, onions, capsicum, chillies and sauté for 2 mins. Add in the tomatoes and mix well. Then add the green chilli sauce, soy sauce, vinegar, ketchup along with red chilli powder, salt and sugar. Stir thoroughly until everything is mixed well. Add water and bring it to a boil.
  3. Once the mixture boils, add the fried fish and black pepper powder. Stir well. Do not overcook it, as the fish will harden.
  4. Garnish with chopped spring onion leaves. Serve hot as a starter with chowmein or Soup.
