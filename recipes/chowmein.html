Chowmein

Description

Chowmein is crazily popular among Bengalis and is sold in almost every corner of Bengal in the carts and stalls by street food vendors. It is also one of the most frequently served Bengali snack recipe by the popular restaurants and hotels in Bengal. Kolkata chowmein is popular because of its unique taste and flavours which invite people to have it repeatedly. This Kolkata chicken chow mein recipe is way different from classic chicken chow mein which you will get in authentic Chinese restaurants.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams Chicken thighs, small pieces (boneless-skinless)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp soya sauce
  • 0.5 tbsp black pepper,salt,cornflour
  • 1tbsp oil for noodles
  • 150 gm noodles
  • 1 tbsp chilli sauce,vinegar
  • 1 small sliced onion, 4 spring onion
  • 1 capsicum
  • 1 medium carrot
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage

Steps

  1. To prepare the Kolkata chowmein, first, wash and clean the chicken thighs and cut them into small pieces. Then transfer the chicken into a mixing bowl. Add salt, pepper, soya sauce, ginger-garlic paste, and corn flour into the bowl. Mix all the ingredients thoroughly and cover the bowl.
  2. Allow the chicken to get marinated for at least 15 minutes.Then take a large non-stick pan and add some oil to it. Once the oil is hot, add marinated chicken pieces into the pan and shallow fry them evenly on medium flame from both sides till brown spots appear and the chicken gets cooked. This step takes around 10 minutes to get cooked. Keep the chicken pieces aside on a plate.
  3. Put a pan on flame and add water and salt to it. Turn the flame high and allow the water to boil. Add the noodles into the pan and cook it on high flame till al dente for around 6 minutes.
  4. In the meantime, take a bowl and add soya sauce, vinegar, tomato ketchup, chilli paste and mix it thoroughly. Keep it aside for later use.Now add some oil into a pan if required. Once the oil is hot, add onion and fry it on high flame for a minute. Then add chopped garlic, spring onion whites and cook it on high flame for another 1 minute.
  5. Add carrot strips and cook them on high flame for a minute. Then add capsicum, cabbage, green chillies into the pan and fry them over high flame for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Now add salt, black pepper, garam masala powder into the pan and mix it nicely. Cook it on high flame for a minute.Add the cooked noodles, fried chicken pieces, spring onion greens, and sauce mixture, one by one into the pan and mix it well. Cook it over high flame for 2-3 minutes and stir it frequently. Then switch off the flame.Your Chicken-chow-mein is ready to be served.
